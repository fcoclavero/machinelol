Dominate the competition
Choose the champion that suits you the best, based on champions that similar users play to win and participate more in the game.

The recomdation comes to you in two steps:

We find similar users through a Clustering Algorithm
We look for your historic characteristics (Total Wins, Total Champion Kills, etc) and run a Clustering Algorithm that makes groups (Clusters) of similar users
based on this characteristics extracted from the LOL Oficial API.

Extract and deliver 5 different rankings
We look into the cluster you belong to, analyzing the information of users of your group. The rankings are computed based in Cluster's champions with most wins 
(Winrate), most participation (KDA) and most used (Champion Mastery Experience); also a personal recomedation is made exploring users that masterize the same
champions as you do delivering a unique ranking of champions made for you.
